Core Corporate Financial Ratios

Valuation Metric Price-to-Earnings (P/E)

Evaluates how expensive a company's stock is relative to its actual earnings per share ($EPS$).

Profitability Metric Return on Capital Employed (ROCE)

Measures how efficiently a company makes profits from both debt and equity combined.

Solvency Metric Debt-to-Equity (D/E)

Checks how heavily a company relies on borrowed money compared to shareholder capital.

Major Candlestick Reversals

Double Candle Pattern Bullish Engulfing

A giant buyer's session completely wraps around the prior selling session, indicating strong institutional entry.

Single Candle Pattern Hammer Structure

Triple Candle Pattern Morning Star Reversal

The ultimate swing transition showing massive selling slowing down to an indecisive crawl, followed by strong buyers.
