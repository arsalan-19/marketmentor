User Persona Mapping & UX Workflows: MarketMentor

This document maps out the core user personas and structural workflows of the MarketMentor platform, with an emphasis on behavioral constraints and safety interlocks.

1. Primary Persona Study

Siddharth (26, Bengaluru) - IT Consultant & Financial Novice

Psychographic Profile: Risk-averse, logical, time-poor, anxious regarding complex finance terminology.

Core Pain Points:

Wants to grow capital but feels overwhelmed by raw chart patterns, candlestick types, and corporate balance sheets.

Frequently encounters "stock tips" on social media but possesses no objective framework to validate them.

Prone to panic-selling or emotional chasing due to daily market volatility.

3. UX Design Guidelines

To maintain alignment with Siddharth’s behavioral profile, the application design follows these visual criteria:

Clean Hierarchy: Visual real estate is prioritized for the plain-language explanation. Complex charts and mathematical ratios are collapsed into clean, accordion-style expansion tabs.

Reduced Color Volatility: Avoid high-contrast flashing colors. Positive changes are soft emerald; negative movements are subdued slate-rose, avoiding panic triggers.

No Redundant Entry Fields: Quantities can be adjusted via simple step increments ($1$, $5$, $10$, $50$ shares) to minimize input fatigue on mobile screens.
