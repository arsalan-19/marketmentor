🏆 MarketMentor: Final Launch & Optimization Checklist

Follow these three final steps directly on GitHub.com to make your repository look exceptionally polished, interactive, and complete for recruiting.

🛠️ Step 1: Link Your Live Website in the "About" Section

Recruiters and hiring managers should not have to hunt for your prototype link in the README. It must be prominently displayed at the very top right of your repository.

Go to your marketmentor repository homepage on GitHub.com.

On the right-hand sidebar, locate the About section and click the gear icon (Settings).

In the Website field, paste your official live GitHub Pages URL:
https://[YOUR_GITHUB_USERNAME].github.io/marketmentor/

In the Topics field, add the following industry-relevant tags to make your profile searchable:
product-management, fintech, wealthtech, android-mvp, mockups, agile, nse-india, clean-architecture

Click Save changes.

📝 Step 2: Set Up Your Agile Backlog & Issues

Demonstrate your developer handoff capabilities by populating your repository's native tracking system.

On your repository homepage, click the Issues tab at the top.

Click the green New Issue button.

Refer to your local docs/agile_backlog_builder.md file:

Issue 1: Copy-paste the title and markdown description for Ticket 1 (Delivery Protection Lock). Add the labels: feature, compliance, high-priority. Click Submit new issue.

Issue 2: Click New Issue again. Copy-paste the details for Ticket 2 (Jargon Buster Tooltips). Add labels: feature, ux, low-priority. Click Submit new issue.

Issue 3: Click New Issue again. Copy-paste the details for Ticket 3 (Dynamic Pre-Market Batching). Add labels: architecture, database, medium-priority. Click Submit new issue.

📋 Step 3: Create Your Public Kanban Project Board

Real product teams live inside sprint boards. You can build a beautiful public workspace board directly on GitHub in under two minutes.

Click the Projects tab at the top of your repository page.

Click the New project button.

Select the Board template (this generates a traditional Kanban card layout with Todo, In Progress, Done columns).

Click Create.

Name your board: MarketMentor Android MVP Roadmap.

Click the Add item button at the bottom of the Todo column, select Add item from repository, and select your 3 newly created Issues.

Drag and drop the cards to simulate an active development cycle:

Move Ticket 1 (Delivery Lock) to the In Progress column.

Move Ticket 3 (Pre-Market Sourcing) to Todo.

Leave Ticket 2 (Jargon Buster) in Todo.

🔍 Step 4: Perform Your Final Quality Audit

Open an incognito browser window and navigate to your public repository URL: https://github.com/[YOUR_GITHUB_USERNAME]/marketmentor.

Check that the README.md formatting looks flawless and that the live prototype link at the very top is clickable and works.

Verify that your Docs folder correctly lists all 5 of your product files (PRD, GTM, User Journeys, Layout Specs, and the setup checklist).

Verify that your Issues and Projects tabs are populated and look like an active developer workspace.
