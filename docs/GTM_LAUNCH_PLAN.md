Go-To-Market & Launch Strategy: MarketMentor

This document establishes the strategic framework for introducing MarketMentor to target retail audiences. It outlines distribution phases, core performance metrics, and tactical feedback loops designed to secure growth with minimal capital expenditure.

1. Value Proposition & Target Segmentation

1.1 Messaging Core

Strategic Position: An educational, algorithmically driven decision-support system that enforces strict, cash-delivery execution boundaries ($T+1$ holding minimums).

Primary Hook: "Ditch the speculative day-trading noise. Master institutional-grade equity analysis with clear narrative breakdowns, and validate your strategy risk-free with $₹10,00,000$ in virtual sandbox capital."

1.2 Target User Profiles

The Paralysed Novice (Primary): Individual holding a newly minted demat account but hesitant to execute due to jargon overload and fear of capital erosion.

The Reforming Speculator (Secondary): Individual transitioning away from high-risk intraday/derivative trading toward disciplined, structural swing equity holding.

Phase 1: Alpha Testing (Internal Validation)

Goal: Verify calculation accuracy of fundamental ratios and live-data parser responsiveness across client devices.

Target: $100$ users drawn from close finance, product management, and engineering cohorts.

Operational focus:

Ensure the daily $08:30$ IST pre-market screening engine caches data correctly.

Test $T+1$ same-day sell restriction enforcement.

Primary Feedback: Direct structured user interviews and bug reporting forms.

Phase 2: Beta Testing (Community Penetration)

Goal: Evaluate user habit loops surrounding the morning dashboard consumption and the usability of the "Why this Stock" NLG templates.

Target: $1,500$ users sourced from programmatic communities, FinTech subreddits (e.g., r/IndiaInvestments), and local wealth-tech channels.

Operational focus:

Track usage peaks (target: $08:45 - 09:30$ IST).

Optimize database lookups to keep server costs under $\$10/\text{month}$.

Primary Feedback: Integration of in-app structured feedback modals and automated crash-logging analytics.

Phase 3: Public Production Release

Goal: Launch native application on Google Play Store; establish systematic organic acquisition loops.

Operational focus:

App Store Optimization (ASO) centered on high-intent search queries: "NSE swing trading assistant," "paper trading India," and "stock fundamental analysis helper."

Feature the prominent delivery-only compliance banners directly in the primary store visual assets.

3. Core Success Metrics & KPI Framework

To demonstrate product management rigor, MarketMentor evaluates performance across three distinct areas of the product funnel:

3.1 Retention & Habituation Metrics

Daily Active Users to Monthly Active Users ($DAU/MAU$) Ratio: Target $>45\%$, indicating high product utility.

Weekly Cohort Retention ($D7$ and $D30$): Target $>35\%$ active retention at day 30.

Morning Session Density: Target average session duration of $4-6$ minutes during market open hours, indicating active consumption of educational content.

3.2 Product Performance Metrics

Feature Adoption Rate: Percentage of users executing a simulated buy within $48$ hours of app installation (Target $>70\%$).

Jargon-Buster Utility: Average number of definition-tooltip activations per session (Target $>3.0$).

System Accuracy (Alpha): Average return rate of "Strong Buy" recommendation buckets relative to the Nifty $50$ index over rolling $90$-day periods.

3.3 Viral Growth Coefficient ($K$-Factor)

Target: $K \ge 0.15$

Mechanic: Provide clean, shareable "Simulated P&L Scorecards" that users can export directly to WhatsApp or LinkedIn to showcase their virtual returns, driving organic referral traffic.
